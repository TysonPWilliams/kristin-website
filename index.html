Our Services

Capacity Building Supports (NDIS)

Personalised support tailored to NDIS goals, with one-on-one therapy, coaching, and written assessments

Mobile Social Work Support

In-home and school-based therapy delivered as part of an integrated, collaborative team approach.

AUTPlay Therapy

Autism-specific play therapy designed for the whole family, including dedicated parent sessions.

Therapeutic Counselling (ACT, CBT, SFT)

Evidence-based therapy for trauma, PTSD, anxiety, and emotional regulation using Acceptance Commitment Therapy, CBT, and Solution-Focused Therapy.

Autism & ADHD Specialist Support

Holistic, neuro-affirming care for individuals and families navigating Autism and ADHD.

Therapeutic Group Work

Small group therapy to build connection, communication, and self-confidence in a safe and supportive environment.

ADHD Coaching & Therapy

Practical strategies and emotional support tailored to living with ADHD, for both children and adults.

Parent Therapy & Behaviour Support

Empowering parents with strategies and guidance to support children with ASD, ADHD, PDA, SPD, and anxiety.
